PDDL stands for **"Privacy, Protection, Data Protection"**. It's a framework used in industries to ensure data privacy and security while protecting sensitive information.

### Key Components of PDDL:
1. **Privacy**: Protects personal, private, or sensitive data.
2. **Protection**: Ensures the confidentiality of systems and data.
3. **Data Protection**: Safeguards against unauthorized access, disclosure, and misuse.

### Objectives:
- **Compliance**: Ensure adherence to laws (e.g., GDPR) regarding data protection.
- **Security**: Protect systems and data from breaches.
- **Transparency**: Communicate requirements clearly with stakeholders.

PDDL is often used in industries like technology, healthcare, finance, and logistics.
